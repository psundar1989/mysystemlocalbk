FAQ's

General

What is BattleLounge?

Battle Lounge is a Video Game Tournament platform. We create and host tournaments for gamers all around the world to compete for prizes and glory! Our tournaments run on PC, Xbox One and Playstation 4.

Do I need to pay money to play on Battle Lounge?

No, payment is not required to engage in our tournaments.

How can I sign up?

You can sign up for Battle Lounge by clicking here or the Sign Up button at the top right of the page. You can sign up for tournaments by visiting one of our game pages or the tournaments page.

How old do I have to be to play?

In order to play in the tournaments on Battle Lounge you must be 18 years of age.

Tournaments

How do the tournaments in Battle Lounge work?

After you create an account with us, you can navigate to the “Games” section and choose from any one of the games that we support on the platform to play a tournament in.

If you would like to see all of the tournaments for all of the games at a given time, navigate to the “Tournaments” tab to view all tournaments for all games. After you find a game and tournament you want to play in, simply click the “Sign Up” button on the tournaments page, adn wait until the tournament begins!

You will be asked to check-in 15 minutes prior to the tournament starting so we know if you will be present to play in the tournament. It is mandatory you check-in in order to play.

After you complete each game, you and your opponent will be asked to submit your scores. You each will agree on the correct score in order to verify the winner. Once the winner has been determined, the tournament will progress you to the next round or to being the champion!

What Games are currently on Battle Lounge?

Right now the current games that we support are Fortnite, Call of Duty Modern Warfare: Warzone, FIFA(20), Apex Legends, Rocket League and NBA 2K(20).

What happens if my internet disconnects during a match?

Different games have different rules regarding disconnect so be sure to check the “Disconnections” portion of the “Rules” section for each game, but typically it will result in a replay of the match as long as it wasn’t deemed intentional.

What happens if my opponent reports the score incorrectly? Or what if my opponent cheats?

Once you complete a match you and your opponent will report the score. If the score is reported incorrectly, or if you feel there was any possibility your opponent cheated, you will have the option to click “Dispute” on the submit scores box. This will allow you to send any videos or images, as well as a text description of why you are disputing the match. We will then review the dispute and make the final judgement.

Note: You are required to give a reasoning for your dispute. If not, you may be subject to a temporary ban for abusing the dispute system. Please only use it if it is necessary.

In order to participate in tournaments that have prizes you must enable 2-step Mobile Verification. This is to verify your account so you do not create multiple accounts on the platform. That’s right...don’t try playing against yourself!
